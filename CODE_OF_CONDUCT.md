# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We strive to provide a friendly, safe, and welcoming environment for everyone, regardless of experience level, gender, identity, or background.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and professional.
- Use inclusive and welcoming language.
- Provide constructive feedback.
- Assume good intentions.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks.
- Discriminatory language or behavior.
- Deliberate disruption of discussions.
- Any form of hate speech.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may take appropriate action, including:

- Warnings
- Temporary or permanent bans from the project space
- Locking or removing toxic discussions

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please open an issue or contact a maintainer privately.

By participating in this project, you agree to abide by this Code of Conduct.
